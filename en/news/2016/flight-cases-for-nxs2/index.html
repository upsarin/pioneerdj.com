23 March, 2016

New flight cases for your NXS2 set-up

Lightweight and robust flight cases for our NXS2 line-up made by 5 Star Cases.

Built with 6-mm industrial plywood and reinforced at the edges with aluminium, the cases are more than a match for life on the road. The foam linings cocoon your NXS2 set-up snugly while leaving enough room for your cables and extras. And the strap handle and new butterfly catches make them easy and secure to carry.

The CDJ-2000NXS2 flight case (FLT-2000NXS2) and the DJM-900NXS2 flight case (FLT-900NXS2) will be available from mid-April, in Europe, the Middle East and Africa only.

KEY FEATURES

  • Available in black
  • Made from industrial 6 mm plywood with bonded high pressure laminate
  • Aluminium reinforced edges
  • Recessed butterfly catches
  • Foam lining on top and bottom of case
  • Strap handle on lid
  • Dust-tight seal
  • Rubber feet
