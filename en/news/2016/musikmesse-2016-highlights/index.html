11 April, 2016

Musikmesse 2016 highlights

Here are some of the highlights of our time at Musikmesse in Frankfurt, including the introduction of the new TORAIZ SP-16 professional sampler, the DM-40 compact desktop speakers, and HRM-5 and HRM-6 monitor headphones. We also enjoyed great showcases by ESKEI83, Jon1st and Jay Frog.

Visit us on Facebook for more Musikmesse highlights

Showcase

  • 06.04.2016 Clarity and comfort: meet the HRM-5 and HRM-6 studio monitor headphones for producers We’re expanding our range of professional studio monitor headphones with the launch of the HRM-5s and HRM-6s. Like the HRM-7s, the new additions draw on our sound reproduction heritage to bring you an accurate, neutral sound that’s perfect for monitoring your latest creations.
  • 05.04.2016 Sample and Sync: the TORAIZ SP-16 sampler with Dave Smith analogue filters and Pro DJ Link for perfectly timed one-shots and loops Take your creativity to another level in the booth, the studio and on stage with the TORAIZ SP-16 sampler and step sequencer. The tactile sampler features an intuitive touch screen and layout, 16 powerful sampler engines, analogue filters based on Dave Smith Instruments’ Prophet-6 synthesiser and quantize via Pro DJ Link. And you can use it as a stand-alone unit – no need to take a laptop into the booth.
  • 01.04.2016 The dual 18-inch, fully horn-loaded XY-218HS with extremely high SPL – ideal for large venues and outdoor events The Pioneer Pro Audio XY Series is getting a powerful addition with the launch of the XY-218HS dual 18-inch, fully horn-loaded subwoofer – designed to deliver ultra-low frequencies at the high sound pressure level (SPL) needed for large venues and outdoor events.
