14 October, 2016

Space Ibiza Final Closing Fiesta - celebrating 27 years of music and magic

After 27 magical years summer 2016 saw the final closing fiesta of the cultural institution that we will forever know in our hearts as Space Ibiza.

We were there for the full 19 hour party with its 20,000 strong crowd and what a celebration it was! Streaming live in collaboration with Be-at.TV on the night; here is our report fitting in as many of the sets as possible including Carl Cox and Nic Fanciulli, along with some very emotional words from an impressive roll call of DJs including Erick Morillo, James Zabiela, Eats Everything, ANNA, Josh Wink, Steve Lawler and more

Cramming 19 hours into 10 minutes was no easy task, we hope you enjoy!!

Soundtrack:
  • Danny Tenaglia and Celada // Music is the Answer - Twisted
  • Skylark // Movin' (Joris Voorn remix) - Saved Records
  • Robert Miles // Children - Smilax Records
  • Mias Void // Return to Bliss (The Model's Dance To Trance Remix) - Born Electric
  • Laurent Garnier // Man with the Red Face - F Communications
