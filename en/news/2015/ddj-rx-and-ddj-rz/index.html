10 September, 2015

Next generation: introducing the DDJ-RZ and DDJ-RX – the world’s first controllers for rekordbox dj

We’re launching the DDJ-RZ and DDJ-RX – the first hardware to deliver native control of rekordbox dj performance software. The controllers give rekordbox DJs the ultimate flexibility and convenience: prepare tracks in rekordbox and then get plug-and-play performance with the software, directly from a laptop.

rekordbox dj is a Plus Pack available to buy from within the latest rekordbox software. Unlocking rekordbox dj gives you access to features including Hot Cues, Sampler, Slicer, Sound Colour FX, Beat FX, Beat Jump and the industry’s first Pad FX. And with the fastest response time, it’s a stable and reliable performer in any environment.

The DDJ-RZ and DDJ-RX mirror the layout of the software precisely to enable intuitive creative performances. Low latency jog wheels with On Jog Display, multi-coloured Performance Pads and a four-channel mixer deliver highly responsive, tactile control. New to these controllers are the beat parameter buttons, Sampler Cue mode, and the Sequencer mode – which lets you record and loop sample sequences on the fly. Plus improved MIC channels deliver a high-quality sound.

The top-flight DDJ-RZ takes the creative possibilities even further, sharing all the features of the DDJ-RX, plus a club-quality magnetic crossfader and large jog wheels inherited from the top-flight CDJ-2000NXS. The OSC Sampler gives you the freedom to trigger and manipulate pre-set oscillator samples – or load your own in rekordbox dj. Two USB sound cards and ports with a top-loaded input switcher enable smooth DJ transitions.

KEY FEATURES

  1. Layout mirrors rekordbox dj for instinctive control
    The DDJ-RZ and DDJ-RX are designed and built to deliver the tightest possible integration and fast response with rekordbox dj.
    • Multi-coloured Performance Pads: 16 large, rubber Performance Pads instantly trigger quantized Hot Cues, Slicer and the new Pad FX.
    • Low latency jog wheels: with On Jog Display for at-a-glance playback and cue information.
    • Four-channel mixer: you can perform with all four of rekordbox dj’s decks, and each channel has its own level meters and trim pots for even greater control. Plus, with multiple input and outputs – including two Master Outs and a Booth Out – the versatile controllers can also be used as stand-alone mixers.
  2. Instant access to a wealth of FX and functions
    Both controllers deliver touch-of-a-button control of rekordbox dj’s FX, plus on-board functions to give you more creative choice. Performance Pads have multi-coloured backlit LEDS to give a visual reference to modes and Hot Cues, while Velocity Mode lets you control the sample volume with how much pressure you apply.
    • PAD FX: you can create multiple FX chains using all 16 pads.
    • Sound Colour FX: the DDJ-RZ’s four FX buttons are pre-set to Space, Jet, Pitch and Filter, while the DDJ-RX’s will trigger Noise, Crush, Pitch and Filter. An FX Plus Pack will be available to buy in the future, allowing you to customise your FX buttons.
    • NEW Sequencer: the rekordbox dj controllers are first to feature the Sequencer, which lets you record, play and loop pad routines on the fly.
    • Release FX: you can exit complex FX patterns by selecting vinyl brake, echo or back spin and twisting the dial.
    • Beat FX: you can select up to three of the software’s quantized Beat FX, then twist the dial to manipulate the parameters of each.
    • Quantized Beat Jump: lets you jump backwards or forwards by any number of beats.
    • Slip Mode: inherited from the CDJ-2000NXS, Slip Mode silently continues playback during a loop, reverse or scratch, then brings the music back at the right point afterwards.
  3. Multiple inputs and outputs for flexible set-ups
    The versatile DDJ-RZ and DDJ-RX have multiple inputs and outputs to give you the flexibility to connect laptops, CDJs and turntables and use the controllers as stand-alone mixers. Plus two Master Outs and a Booth Out ensure high-quality connections with PA systems.
  4. Other features
    • Professional build quality
    • High-quality audio circuitry
    • Crossfader curve adjust
    • Fader Start function
    • Needle Search
    • High-quality MIC input

DDJ-RZ: NEW FEATURES

  1. OSC Sampler for enhanced manipulation of sound
    The DDJ-RZ features an oscillator sampler, which allows you to overlay four pre-set sounds – Noise, Sine, Siren and Horn – and modulate the output using the dial. Or you can load your own sounds to the banks in rekordbox dj.
  2. 2 USB ports for seamless DJ handovers
    Two sound cards and USB ports plus a top-loaded input switcher mean two laptops can be connected at the same time for seamless DJ transitions.
  3. Durable, responsive magnetic crossfader
    The DDJ-RZ is equipped with our own magnetic crossfader for smooth, steady control. Two metal shafts support the fader, while the contact-free magnetic system is designed to last for millions of performances.
  4. Other features
    • Jog Feeling Adjust lets you set your preferred resistance on the jog wheels
    • P-Lock Fader Cap stops fader cap coming off during energetic performances
    • Auto standby switches the controller off after a period of inactivity
